
###################### USERS ######################

1) Register User: [POST] http://localhost:8080/users/register

2) Login User: [POST] http://localhost:8080/users/login

3) LogOut User: [GET] http://localhost:8080/user/logout

4) GetAll Users: [GET] http://localhost:8080/users/


###################### DOCTORS ######################

1) GetAll Doctor: [GET] http://localhost:8080/doctors/

2) Add Doctor: [POST] http://localhost:8080/doctors/add

3) Update Doctor: [PATCH] http://localhost:8080/doctors/update/:id

4) Delete Doctor: [DELETE] http://localhost:8080/doctors/delete/:id
